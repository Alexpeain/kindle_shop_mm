↩️ Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. Returns

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and in the same condition you received them.

2. Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed within 3–5 business days via the original payment method.

3. Non-Returnable Items

Items that are damaged due to misuse, or items returned after 7 days will not be eligible for a refund.

4. How to Request a Return

Contact us at your@email.com or message us on Facebook with your order details and reason for return. We will guide you through the process.

5. Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item arrived damaged or defective.
